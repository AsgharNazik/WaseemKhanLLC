Trading Overview
Trading involves buying and selling financial assets like stocks, forex, and Cryptocurrencies with primary objective of generating profit. Successful traders rely on Technical Analysis, risk management strategies and a disciplined approach to navigate the complexities of the financial markets. They analyze price charts, identify trends, and make informed decisions based on market data.
Trading Types
Types
Short Term Trading: Involves making quick trades to capitalize on short-term price movements.
Mid term Trading: Involves holding positions for days or weeks to capture medium-term trends.
Long Term Trading: Involves holding positions for months or years to benefit from long-term market trends.
